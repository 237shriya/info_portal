Competency Development Programme

Our Competency Development Programme in Network and Mobile Security is designed to equip participants with advanced skills in cybersecurity. This programme covers various aspects of network security, mobile security, threat analysis, and mitigation strategies to ensure a comprehensive understanding of modern security challenges.

Participants will engage in hands-on workshops, real-world scenarios, and expert sessions to develop their proficiency in protecting and securing network infrastructures and mobile applications. The programme aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
